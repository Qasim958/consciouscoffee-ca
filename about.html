About Us

We are a B Corp Leading the World toward Better Business

We are a Certified B Corp. We meet rigorous standards of social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency. It takes a village to build a business, and we believe it is good business to serve our community in turn! That’s why we’re proud to lead our fellow B Corps in working to improve the quality of life for those around us as a 2022 Best for the World Honoree— the seventh time we’ve received this award! We earned a community impact score in the top 5% of all Certified B Corporations internationally on the B Impact Assessment—a comprehensive assessment of a company’s impact on workers, the community, and the environment. It’s an honor to lead a global movement to redefine success in business so that one day all companies will compete to not only be the best in the world but also to be best for the world.
